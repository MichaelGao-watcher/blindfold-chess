Basics

Everything you need to get started.

Board Coordinates

A chessboard has 8 files (columns a–h) and 8 ranks (rows 1–8). Each square is named by its file letter plus rank number, like e4 or d5.

Algebraic Notation

Moves are written using algebraic notation: e4 (pawn to e4), Nf3 (knight to f3), O-O (short castle), Qxd5 (queen takes on d5).

Blindfold Chess

In blindfold chess, you play without seeing the board. You call out moves in notation, and your opponent (or the engine) replies the same way. Keeping the entire position in your mind is the challenge.
